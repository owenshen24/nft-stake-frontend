yfλ (yf Lambda) is an unofficial contract for staking your yfβ to earn dynamic NFTs. You can learn more about yfβ here.

Here's how to get started:

  1. Head to the Stake tab and go to Manage Stake on the right card. Enter in how much yfβ you want to stake, and then click Stake.
  2. After the transaction is confirmed, you can mine a Gem that represents your stake. Click the Mine Gem button and approve the transaction.
  3. To withdraw your stake at any time, click the Withdraw All button. Gems represent your staked amount, so be aware that hitting Withdraw will cause your Gem to stop growing.
  4. Once you've mined a Gem, you can see it in the Gem tab. Gems are ERC-721 compliant, so you can also trade and view them on OpenSea.
