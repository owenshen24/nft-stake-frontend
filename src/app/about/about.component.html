About

yfλ (yf Lambda) is an unofficial contract for staking your yfβ to earn βetagems, Dynamic NFTs. You can learn more about yfβ here. This means that the longer you stake, the more your NFT will evolve. Check back often to see your Gems take on new forms and colors! The staking contract can be found here on Etherscan.


Getting Started

  1. Head to the Stake tab and go to Manage Stake on the right card. Enter in how much yfβ you want to stake, and then click Stake.
  2. After the transaction is confirmed, you can mine a βetagem that represents your staked yfβ. Click the Mine Gem button and approve the transaction.
  3. To withdraw your stake at any time, click the Withdraw All button. βetagem represent your staked amount, so be aware that clicking Withdraw All will cause your βetagems to stop growing!
  4. Once you've mined a βetagems, you can see it in the βetagems tab
