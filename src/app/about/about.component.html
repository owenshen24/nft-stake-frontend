About

yfλ (yf Lambda) is an unofficial contract for staking your yfβ to earn Dynamic NFTs. You can learn more about yfβ here. This means that, the longer you stake, the more your NFT will evolve. Check back often to see your Gems take on new forms and colors! The staking contract can be found here on Etherscan.


Getting Started

  1. Head to the Stake tab and go to Manage Stake on the right card. Enter in how much yfβ you want to stake, and then click Stake.
  2. After the transaction is confirmed, you can mine a Gem that represents your staked yfβ. Click the Mine Gem button and approve the transaction.
  3. To withdraw your stake at any time, click the Withdraw All button. Gems represent your staked amount, so be aware that clicking Withdraw All will cause your Gem to stop growing!
  4. Once you've mined a Gem, you can see it in the Gem tab

What's next for the project?

I'll be continuing front-end updates, and staked tokens will continue to have their NFTs evolve over time.

Also, I'll be working on integrations with other platforms like Rarible and OpenSea so the dynamism can transfer over to other platforms. You can already trade Gems because Gems are ERC-721 compliant, but it will take some more work to get the evolving images to update elsewhere.
